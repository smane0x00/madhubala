About Madhubala

Born as Mumtaz Jehan Begum Dehlavi, Madhubala became one of the greatest stars of Hindi cinema. She appeared in over 70 films and is best remembered for her role in "Mughal-e-Azam".

Her expressive acting, elegance, and timeless beauty earned her the title "Venus of Indian Cinema".

Despite a short life, her legacy continues to inspire generations.
